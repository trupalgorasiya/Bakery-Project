FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS!

You should place it 3-4 weeks before the event. Or you may place it even before that. The earlier, the better!

Yes, we make eggless cakes as well. Along with cakes, we have eggless pastries, breads, donuts, etc. as well!
What all items do you make?
We make cakes, pastries, cupcakes, cookies, donuts, waffles, breads and many more items. You can refer to them in the items section on the home page of our website. Happy treats! :)
Do you take care of the hygiene while preparing the items?
Yes! Hygiene is our first priority. We take great care of it while preparing the items.
